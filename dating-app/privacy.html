Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dating App ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the practices described here. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account identifiers. Email address, phone number, or Apple ID (used only for sign-in).
  • Profile data. Display name, birth date, gender, sexual orientation / interested-in, bio, prompts, photos, approximate location (city/country).
  • User content. Messages you send to matches or AI personas, reports you file.
  • Payment references. Apple In-App Purchase transactions (handled by Apple; we receive only anonymized receipt references via RevenueCat).

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Precise location. When you grant permission, we use your device's coordinates to compute distance to other profiles. We store only a coarse point (typically rounded to the city/region level) and last-active timestamp.
  • Device information. Device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) device token.
  • Usage data. Screens viewed, swipes, matches, message counts, feature interactions. Used for analytics, fraud prevention, and product improvement.
  • Diagnostic logs. Crash reports and performance traces (via Sentry, if enabled in your build).

1.3 Information we do not collect

  • Contacts, SMS, browsing history outside the app, or microphone / camera recordings without your explicit action.
  • Precise GPS coordinates of your live location — only the point you upload at setup time or manual refresh.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and maintain your account; authenticate you across devices.
  • Match you with other users based on your preferences and proximity.
  • Deliver messages, match notifications, and product updates via Apple Push Notifications.
  • Operate the AI Practice feature (process your messages with our AI model provider to generate responses).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, harassment, and illegal activity.
  • Moderate photos and text content for safety (automated + manual review).
  • Process in-app purchases and subscription entitlements.
  • Understand how the Service is used so we can improve it.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. International Transfers

Your information is stored and processed primarily in the United States. If you are outside the US, your information is transferred there for processing. By using the Service you consent to such transfers.

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful mechanisms.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts. We retain your data while your account is active.
  • Account deletion. When you delete your account (Profile → Delete account), we permanently remove your profile, photos, matches, messages, AI chats, and auth record, typically within 30 days. Some information may persist in secure backups for up to 90 days before routine expiry.
  • Safety-related data. Where needed to enforce our Terms or respond to a safety incident, we may retain limited information (such as a hashed device identifier and abuse report) for up to 12 months, or longer if required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data (edit in Profile → Edit profile).
  • Delete your data (Profile → Delete account).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Portability — request an export of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and to opt out of "sales" (we do not sell). EU/UK residents have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR.

Email cuongproit@gmail.com to exercise any of the above.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 17. If we learn a user is under 17, we will terminate the account and delete the associated data.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures including TLS 1.2+ in transit, encryption at rest for stored data, row-level security on our database, and access controls on our server-side systems. No method of transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. AI Practice Transparency

  • Every AI persona on the Practice tab is an AI. They disclose this when asked.
  • Messages you send to an AI persona are transmitted to our model provider (currently Anthropic) to produce a response. The provider does not use your messages to train their base models under our API agreement.
  • AI conversations are stored on our backend to maintain context and enforce daily usage caps. They are deleted when you delete your account.
  • Nothing you say to an AI persona is shown to real users on the Dating tab.

10. Apple Privacy Labels

We submit and maintain the following privacy "nutrition labels" in the App Store:

  • Data Linked to You: Contact info, user content (photos, chat), identifiers, purchases, usage data, diagnostics.
  • Data Not Collected: Browsing history, search history, contacts, health, financial info, sensitive info.
  • Data Used to Track You: None.
